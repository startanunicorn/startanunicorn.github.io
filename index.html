Start a Unicorn provides a structured learning path for individuals driven by technology but lacking traditional tech education. We focus on building a strong foundation in algorithmic thinking *before* diving into code.

The Tech Skills Gap is Real.

Many talented individuals are eager to enter the tech industry but face barriers. Traditional education can be expensive and time-consuming. Online resources often jump straight into coding without building the necessary foundational knowledge.

Our Solution: Algorithms First, Code Second.

Start a Unicorn offers a different approach. We guide you through a carefully designed curriculum that prioritizes:

  • Algorithmic Thinking Exercises: Develop problem-solving skills without the pressure of code.
  • Pseudocode Mastery: Learn to express solutions logically before translating them into a specific programming language.
  • Progressive Code Learning: Apply your solid foundation to learn coding more effectively and efficiently.

This approach builds a deeper understanding, making you a more versatile and adaptable tech professional.

Benefits of Learning with Start a Unicorn:

  • Gain in-demand skills for a rewarding tech career.
  • Increase your earning potential and open doors to new opportunities.
  • Learn at your own pace, with a flexible and accessible online platform.
  • Develop a strong foundation that makes learning *any* programming language easier.
  • Become a more confident and effective problem-solver.
